ABOUT US

Tony Pepperoni's was established in 1967 by husband and wife Antonio 'Tony Pepperoni' Romano and Mariana De Lucia-Romano, shortly after they imigrated to Manhattan from a small vilage in the Italian countryside. They had four children, and following Tony's retirement in 1993, his eldest son, Mateo Antonio 'Tony Perroni Jr.' Romano took over the business. Today, the restaurant is managed by Tony Jr. alongside his sister, Gabriella Antonia "Toni" Romano-Snachez. Together, the siblings have expanded the business and opened four franchise restaurants across New York, with plans for morein progress. To this day, the siblings still use their father's original recipes, and hold all their restaurants to the same high standards of quality, service, and taste that their father established all those years ago.
